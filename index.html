Hello, I am Vivian Liu! A UX/UI designer living in Orange County.

With my background in graphics and packaging design, I have enjoyed a successful career as a visual designer, but fell in love with UX/UI design after enrolling in a UX/UI design boot camp program. I love learning about users and their needs and delving into the thought process behind UX/UI design. With my background in graphic design, I have solid experience in problem solving, and know how to create engaging, user-friendly, and functional products. A meticulous design enthusiast, I love playing with colors, and typography and imagery are my biggest passion.

In my spare time, I enjoy cooking, pilates and walking with my four-legged kid Heihei.
