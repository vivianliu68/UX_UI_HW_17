ABOUT

With my background in graphics and packing design, I prospered a successful career in visual design. But after enrolling in UC Irvine’s UX/UI Design Boot Camp Program, I came to have a love for learning about User Centered Design and Process. With my design process, I have a solid foundational skills in problem solving towards creating user-friendly, and functional products. As a meticulous design enthusiast, I love playing with colors, typography, and imagery: and these are just few of my passions!

In my spare time, I enjoy cooking, pilates, and walking my four-legged doggy HeiHei
